Print objects

Print objects with base::print(), and use different arguments for different objects.

X = c('a', 'b', 'c', 'c', 'c', 'a')
Y = factor(c('A', 'B', 'C', 'C', 'D', 'E'))
Y  # factor

#> [1] "A" "B" "C" "C" "D" "E"
#> 5 Levels: "A" "B" ... "E"

table(X, Y)  # table

#>    Y
#> X   A B C D E
#>   a 1 . . . 1
#>   b . 1 . . .
#>   c . . 2 1 .
